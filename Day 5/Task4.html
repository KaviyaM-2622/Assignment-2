Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a type of technology that helps machines think and learn like humans. It is used in many areas of our lives, such as mobile phones, healthcare, education, and transportation. Voice assistants like Siri and Alexa use AI to understand what we say. In hospitals, AI helps doctors find diseases and suggest treatments. Self-driving cars use AI to move safely without a driver. It also helps companies work faster and make better decisions. AI is growing quickly and is changing the way we live, work, and communicate every day.
