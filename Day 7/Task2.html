Steps to Cook Chicken Biryani

Instruction
  1. Wash and marinate the chicken with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and biryani masala.
  2. Soak the basmati rice in water for about 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies, then cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's half done.
  6. Boil the soaked rice until it is 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken and sprinkle fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves.
  8. Add a few drops of saffron milk or food color on top for flavor and color.
  9. Cover with a lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  10. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or boiled eggs.
